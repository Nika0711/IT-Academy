Best Jewelry Stores in Paris

1Shares
122220

We all love and appreciate luxury jewelry and some of the best jewelry stores are situated in Paris. The city of lights always has very important events during the year, like Maison et Object in Septembre and January so it’s important places as Place Vendôme, this area is famous for high fashion and the best jewelry design house boutiques is a like a Parisian Jewelry Box, interesting for people who came to Paris to participate in this events.

Take a look of this top seven jewelry boutiques and stay inspired!

CARTIER – 23 place Vendôme, Paris, France 

Timeless and needs no introductions. designs, manufactures, distributes and sells jewelry and watches. Founded in Paris, France in 1847 by Louis-François Cartier, the company remained under family control until 1964.

The company maintains its headquarters in Paris. Cartier will always remain a fabulous inspirational avenue for me and all jewelry design houses around the world.

DUBAIL- 21 Place Vendôme, Paris, France

Pierre Dubail has developed several lines of jewelry, taking care to choose, all over the world, the most beautiful precious stones.

Each piece is unique, perfect and meticulously worked with few artisans at the top of their art.

MIKIMOTO–  8 Place Vendôme, Paris, France

This brand is known for their work with fresh water pearls. The designer, Kokichi Mikimoto, is a visionary on a quest for beauty, it was his dream to “adorn the necks of all women around the world with pearls”.

Since his company’s founding in 1893, elegant women worldwide have been entranced by cultured pearl, mysterious gems of the sea.

See also: Millionaire Collection by Boca do Lobo

CHANEL- 18 Place Vendôme, Paris, France

No introduction needed here. Chanel represents timeless classic beauty and in my opinion, is the ultimate symbol of femininity.

BVLGARI- 25 Place Vendôme, Paris, France

I have always loved this design house. Sotirios Voulgaris began his career as a jeweler in his home village in Greece. where his first store can still be seen.

In 1877, he left for Corfu and then Naples. In 1881 he finally moved to Rome, where in 1884 he founded his company and opened his second shop in via Sistina.

VAN CLEEF AND ARPELS- 22/24 Place Vendôme, Paris, France

The story of Van Cleef and Arpels began when Alred Van Cleef married Estelle Arpels in Paris, in 1895. The Maison was founded at 22 Place Vendôme in 1906, in a 18th-century mansion.

Van Cleef & Arpels reputation grew thanks to its cosmopolitan clientele and its prescience at prestigious events like the 1925 Exposition Internationale des Arts Decoratifs. I will be sharing much more information about this fabulous place with you soon.

BOUCHERON- 26 Place Vendôme, Paris, France

The House of Boucheron is a French family dynasty founded by Frederic Boucheron in 1858, with the opening of his first store in the Galerie de Valois. In 1893, Frédéric Boucheron became the first jeweler to move to Place Vendôme.

Legend has it that he chose 26 Place Vendôme, where Boucheron remains to this day because it was the sunniest corner of the square. He believed that the diamonds in the windows would sparkle all the more brilliantly.
